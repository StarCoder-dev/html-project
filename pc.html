Introduction

Core Architecture

  • CPU: x86 and ARM chips execute instructions.
    Reference: Wikipedia
  • Memory: DRAM modules store active data for rapid access.
    Reference: Wikipedia
  • Storage: HDDs and NVMe SSDs provide long-term data retention.
    Reference: Wikipedia
  • Motherboard: Integrates chipset, expansion slots, and power delivery.
    Reference: Wikipedia
  • Graphics: Integrated GPUs or discrete graphics cards render images.
